Strength Training

Includes squats, push-ups, and weightlifting. Recommended: 3–4 sets of 8–12 reps per exercise.

Benefits: builds muscle, increases strength, and boosts metabolism.

Cardio Workouts

Activities like running, cycling, and jump rope. Aim for 30 minutes daily for optimal cardiovascular health.

Flexibility Exercises

Includes yoga, stretching, and balance training. Helps improve mobility and reduces the risk of injuries.
