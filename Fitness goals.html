How to Set Your Goals

Start by defining a clear objective (e.g., lose 5 kg in two months), and break it down into weekly milestones to stay on track.

Protein Commitment

Consume between 1.6 and 2.2 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight. For example, someone weighing 75 kg should aim for around 150g of protein daily.

Sources include: chicken, fish, yogurt, and supplements like whey protein.
